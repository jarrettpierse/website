COURTVILLE ANTIQUES

Photography & Editing

Role Digital Photography, Social Media & Content Creation
Company Courtville Antiques Ltd.

This project consisted of ongoing work for Courtville Antiques Ltd. to produce digital assets for their website and social media. The photographic work was mostly with jewellery, as

well as with silverware and artwork. All photos were taken in a controlled studio environment to reduce reflections and improve the quality of the work. Post-processing work was

done using Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom to create the finishing images. The photographed images were then used

to promote Courtville Antiques both on their website and over social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram.
