YVOLUTION

Videography & Editing

Role Digital Marketing, Graphics & Innovation
Company Yvolve Sports Ltd.

I was tasked with filming, producing and editing over 30 assesmbly instruction videos for Yvolve Sports Ltd. This project was completed over the course of several weeks. In order to create the final product, all filming had to be carried

out in a studio environment on site. Composition and clarity were essential to meet the aims of the brief: to create informative and useful instructional videos to assist customers and improve their overall product experience.

The process of composing and filming individual shots consumed the majority of my time. Following this was a complete video editing process that required working with

over 40GB of footage. The final video segments were sent to a professional voiceover actor who recorded audio which was then added over the video files to create the final product.
